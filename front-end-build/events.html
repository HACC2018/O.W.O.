PAST / UPCOMING WASTE AUDITS

University of Hawaii at Manoa

8:30 am to 1:30 pm at Campus Center on 09/21/18

WHAT

The University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa is conducting an audit designed to highlight the reality of the campus waste stream for students, faculty and administrators, while providing valuable data to empower better decision-making.

WHO

Students, faculty, staff and other community stakeholders. The entire UH Mānoa community is invited to participate.

HOW

Sorting, categorizing and weighing waste, then recording and analyzing data. The audit targets specific types of waste (i.e. plastic utensils, disposable beverage containers) to enable a more targeted response from the UH community.

WHY

This is only the second campus-wide waste audit in almost 20 years. It is an important step in formulating UH Mānoa’s waste reduction goals and strategies.

CONTACT

Nicole Chatterson, (808) 561-7730 Student Sustainability Coordinator, UH Office of Sustainability
